Share files

Sharing files can improve your work.

In Famedly it is possible to share various files.

Share files in Famedly​

  • Desktop
  • Mobile
  1. Open a group or private chat you want to share the file with.
  2. Click on the Paperclip icon.
  3. Select the file you want to share and confirm.
  1. Open a group or private chat you want to share the file with.
  2. Click on the Paperclip icon.
  3. Select Document.
  4. Select the file you want to share and confirm.

Share images and videos​

  • Desktop
  • Mobile
  1. Open a group or private chat you want to share the picture or video with.
  2. Click on the paperclip icon.
  3. Select the image/video from your file system.
  4. Click Yes to confirm sharing.
  1. Open a group or private chat you want to share the picture or video with.
  2. Tap Video, Gallery or Camera.
  3. Select a picture/video or take a picture/video with the camera.
  4. Optional: Tap Black icon if you want to blacken parts of the picture.
  5. Tap the Checkmark icon.
info

Files can also be dragged and dropped into the chat room where they would like to be sent.

Download files and pictures​

You are able to download files and pictures that you received in a chat.

  • Desktop
  • Mobile
  1. Open the chat with the file you want to download.
  2. Click on the file, then on the download arrow in the top right corner.
  3. Confirm the notification.
  1. Open the chat with the file you want to download.
  2. Click on the file an then on the arrow in the top right corner.
  3. Confirm the notification.

Make image sections unrecognizable​

  • Mobile
  1. Follow the steps of sharing an image.
  2. Before tapping the Checkmark icon you can select the colors black or white by clicking on the small painter scroll icon in the bottom right corner of the app when you have selected an picture.
  3. Tap the Checkmark icon after you are finished.
