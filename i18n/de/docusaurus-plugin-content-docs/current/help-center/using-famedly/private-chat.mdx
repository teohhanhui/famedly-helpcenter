## Privatchats starten

Um Privatchats mit einer Person zu starten, folgen Sie den folgenden Schritten.

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>
  <ol>
    <li>Klicke auf den <b>Chat starten</b> Button in der oberen linken Ecke der App.</li>
    <li>Klicken Sie auf <b>Chat starten</b>.</li>
    <li>Klicken Sie auf den Namen der Person, mit der Sie einen Privatchat starten möchten.</li>
    <li>Klicken Sie auf <b>Chat starten</b> oder <b>Chat öffnen</b>.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile">
  <ol>
    <li>Tippen Sie auf <b>Chats</b> am unteren Bildschirmrand.</li>
    <li>Tippen Sie auf die <b>+ Neu</b> Schaltfläche unten rechts auf Ihrem Bildschirm.</li>
    <li>Tippen Sie auf den Namen der Person, mit der Sie einen Privatchat starten möchten.</li>
    <li>Tippen Sie auf das <b>Sprechblasen-Symbol</b>.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

:::info

Wenn Sie nicht die Person finden, mit der Sie einen privaten Chat starten möchten, schauen Sie bitte hier nach.

:::

## Join Private Chat

To join a Private Chat another person would need to start a Private Chat with you first. If the other person has started a Private Chat with you, there is an invitation to join waiting for you.

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>
  <ol>
    <li>You can only join private chats if you have received an invitation.</li>
    <li>All invitations can be found at the top of the chats list.</li>
    <li>Click on the private chats you have been invited to.</li>
    <li>Accept the invitation.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile">
  <ol>
    <li>You can only join private chats if you have received an invitation.</li>
    <li>All invitations can be found at the top of the chats list.</li>
    <li>Tap on the private chats you have been invited to.</li>
    <li>Accept the invitation.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

:::danger

If you decline the invitation, you will have to ask the person again to stat a private chat with you, or you can start the private chat.

:::

## Leave Private Chat

You have always the possibilty to leave a Private Chat. Please take care, that you would need to start a new Private Chat again to continue the conversation with the other person.

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>
  <ol>
    <li>Click on the <b>three dots</b> in the top right corner of a private chat’s screen to open the group details.</li>
    <li>Click on <b>Leave Conversation</b>.</li>
    <li>Click <b>Exit</b>.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile">
  <ol>
    <li>Tap the header of a private chat to open the privat chat details.</li>
    <li>Tap <b>Exit Chat</b> at the bottom of the page.</li>
    <li>Select <b>Yes</b>.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

:::note

With leaving the Private Chat, the conversation will be archived for you. You can find the archive in the settings to read the past conversation.

:::

## Delete a Private Chat

To delete a Private Chat you need to leave the room first. After this follow the steps below to delete the Private Chat. You will always delete the Private Chat for yourself - the other person will still have access to the past conversations.

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>
  <ol>
    <li>Click on your <b>profile picture or name initials</b> to the right of the filter field to open the settings.</li>
    <li>Click on <b>Archive</b>.</li>
    <li>Click the <b>Checkmark icon</b> in the top right corner of the screen.</li>
    <li>Select one or more private chats you want to delete.</li>
    <li>Click the <b>Bin icon</b>.</li>
    <li>Click <b>Yes.</b></li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile">
  <ol>
    <li>Tap <b>Profile Picture or Name Initials</b> at the top right of the screen to open the settings.</li>
    <li>Tap <b>Archive</b>.</li>
    <li>Tap <b>Empty archive</b>.</li>
    <li>Tap <b>Delete</b> to remove all private chats & group chats.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

:::note

Loading the archive may take some time.

:::

## Mute Private Chat

If you do not want get disturbed by a certain Private Chat, you can mute it.

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>
  <ol>
    <li>Click on the <b>three dots</b> in the top right corner of a privat chat to open the private chat details.</li>
    <li>Click on the <b>Bell icon</b> to mute the private chat.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile">
  <ol>
    <li>Tap the header of a privat chat to open the privat chat details.</li>
    <li>Tap the <b>Bell iocn</b> to mute the group.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

:::note

You can unmute the Private Chat again, by following the same steps and clicking the crossed out <b>Bell icon</b>.

:::
