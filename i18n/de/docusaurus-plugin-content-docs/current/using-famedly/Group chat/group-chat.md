# Delete a group

To save disk space on your device, you can delete archived groups. To be able to delete a group on the device, you must first leave it.

<aside>
üöß It is currently not possible to delete a group for all members as an administrator.

</aside>

<aside>
üöß Note: Loading the archive may take some time

</aside>

<details>
<summary>Desktop</summary>

1. Click on your **profile picture or name initials** to the right of the filter field to open the settings.
2. Click on **Archive**.
3. Click ‚òë in the top right corner of the screen.
4. Select one or more groups you want to delete.
5. Click üóë.
6. Click **Yes.**

</details>


<details>
<summary>Desktop</summary>

</details>
